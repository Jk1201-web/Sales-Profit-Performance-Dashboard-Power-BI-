# Sales Profit Performance Dashboard Power-BI
## Overview
This project analyzes how discount strategies impact sales, profit, and overall business performance using Power BI. The goal is to identify an optimal pricing strategy that maximizes profitability.
## Problem Statement
Businesses often increase discounts to boost sales, but excessive discounting can lead to significant losses. This project explores the relationship between discount levels and profitability.
## Dashboard Preview
- ![Sales_performance_dashboard.png](Sales_performance_dashboard.png)
## Key Metrics
1. *Total Sales*: `$2.30M`
2. *Total Profit*: `$286.40K`
3. *Total Orders*: `9,994`
4. *Profit Margin*: `12.47%`
## Impact Analysis (Key Highlight)
- Profit Margin improved from **-180.03%** → **16.61%** (+196% improvement)
- Profit shifted from **-$30.54K (loss)** → **$9.03K (profit)**
- Sales increased by **~220%** at optimized *discount level*
- **Lower discounts resulted in higher profitability despite fewer orders**
## Key Insights
1. High discount levels (>50%) lead to severe negative profit margins
2. Optimal discount range is 10%–20%
3. All regions show stable profitability at lower discounts
4. Sales growth does not always translate into profit
5. Some products remain unprofitable even at low discounts
## Business Recommendations
- Maintain discount levels between **10%–20%**
- Avoid excessive discounting **(>50%)**
- Focus on high-margin products and regions
- Re-evaluate pricing strategy for loss-making items
